To my delight, in 2021, Jan founded the Mitford Museum in Hudson, NC in the renovated school building where she attended first through seventh grade. One of a handful of museum objectives is "To serve as a physical and online worldwide gathering place for fans of the Mitford series." At the Museum visitors can drop into Happy Endings Bookstore and purchase books, gifts, home decor and many other items; an exhibit dedicated to Jan's childhood, complete with a replica of her first grade classroom, a cherished doll she received from her mother when she was four years old, her grandmother's milking stool, the family's butter churn and so much more. Naturally, the museum holds the GetLit! literary lab each September for creative writers and readers to connect, attend workshops, participate in writing contests take advantage of museum tours and generally be inspired and encouraged. The Spring tea is a full afternoon affair with Jan in attendance and opportunities to shop at Happy Endings and a silent auction. The proceeds from the tea support the anticipated Mitford Discovery Center which will have the oral history library plus more exhibitions and activities. A unique feature is the Oral History Library where guests can record their oral histories to be archived
