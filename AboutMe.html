Why Coding?

In 2019 I was awarded an Adult Learner scholarship from KC Scholars, now Great Jobs KC. Originally, the scholarship was renewable for five years. However, along the way, another year was added. And...I lost my job. The term "Learn to code" suddenly had meaning. Not that I had a new desire to become a web developer, but I was faced with the reality of the changing world. So, I thought I would stretch myself and learn something completely new, since I now have more time and scholarship funds. I decided to enroll in the html course at JCCC.The course will not count towards the completion of my A.A. this summer, but it made sense to enroll, considering it's being paid for with my scholarship. "Stretch" is an understatement! My mind has been expanding far beyond anything I could have dreamed. Although I have no plans to become a web developer, I am very proud that I have been able to gain some rudimentary coding skills and will be able to make a meaningful contribution to my family's reunion website.
