Mitford Book Series Order

Any one of the Mitford books is enjoyable as a standalone, but it is fun to "grow" along with the characters, reading them in order of publication.

Ms. Karon did a brilliant thing. She extracted a few of the stories from the larger series and wrote separate books. Home to Holly Springs is my favorite!
